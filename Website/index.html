What's the Game of Thrones Thematic Park?

Game of Thrones Thematic Park is a awesome adventure within the world of the American fantasy drama television series created by David Benioff and D. B. Weiss for HBO which is an adaptation of A Song of Ice and Fire, a series of fantasy novels by George R. R. Martin, the first of which is A Game of Thrones. It tells the story of nine noble families wage war against each other in order to gain control over the mythical land of Westeros. Meanwhile, a force is rising after millenniums and threatens the existence of living men.
You will be able to explore different areas from the North and cold of Winterfell to the South of Dorne. In these different there are a bunch of avtivies waiting for you such as horse rides, thematic dragon roler coasters, castles tours and a lot more that you can take a look in the section below.
